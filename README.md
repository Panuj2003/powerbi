Dashboard Overview

The dashboard provides an at-a-glance view of:

Total Sales

Courier Status Distribution

Top-5 Most Selling Categories

Fulfillment Partners

Shipment State Distribution

Shipment Size Distribution

Date Range Filtering

All visuals are displayed on a single interactive dashboard page.

Key Insights

Total sales amount is 11.58M, indicating strong sales performance.

Shipped orders = 84.88%, showing excellent delivery success rate.

T-shirt category recorded the highest sales count, followed by trousers.

Ekart and Easy Ship are major fulfillment partners.

Amazon Fulfilled orders are significantly higher than Merchant Fulfilled orders.

Most shipped clothing sizes are M, L, and XL, indicating high demand for mid-to-large sizes.

Telangana, Uttar Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, and West Bengal are the top shipping states by order volume.

Conclusion

This dashboard helps quickly identify:

Top-performing categories

Regions with high order volume

The efficiency of courier and fulfillment partners

Customer purchase behavior based on size and product type

It demonstrates strong visualization and storytelling skills using Power BI.
